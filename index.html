About Us

VB Film Makers is an established film and television production company in India, set up in 1995. The company is actively involved in film production and television serials distributed across the world.

We undertake Short Films, Feature Films, Advertisement Films, Corporate Films and Documentary projects. Strong importance is given to social themes blended with entertainment value.

Our short films have succeeded in many international film festivals, and our projects have won national and international awards.

R. Bhuvana (Buvana Ram)

R. Bhuvana is the Founder, Producer, Director and Distributor of VB Film Makers. She has completed professional filmmaking courses at the University of Melbourne and Los Angeles, USA.

She began her career as a journalist and evolved into a complete media professional capable of writing, directing and executing projects independently.

She undertakes projects on a first-copy basis and handles national and international productions with strong creative and technical expertise.
