About Us

VB Film Makers is an established film and television production company in India set up in 1995. We are actively involved in Film Production and Television Serials which are distributed all over the world.

We undertake Short Films, Feature Films, Ad Films, Corporate Films and Documentary projects. Our productions give strong importance to social themes blended with entertainment value.

Our short films have succeeded in many international film festivals, and our projects have won several national and international awards.

About R. Bhuvana

R. Bhuvana (Buvana Ram) is the Founder, Producer and Director of VB Film Makers. She has completed professional film making courses at the University of Melbourne and Los Angeles, United States.

She began her career as a journalist and later evolved into a versatile media professional. She can write, direct and execute complete media projects independently.

She undertakes projects on a first-copy basis and has extensive experience in handling national and international productions.

Producer | Director | Distributor

As a producer, she focuses on meaningful cinema with global relevance. As a director, she blends realism with strong storytelling. As a distributor, she ensures international festival circulation and worldwide reach.
